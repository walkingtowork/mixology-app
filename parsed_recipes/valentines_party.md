# Parsed Drink Recipes - Valentine's Party Menu

## 1. 21st Century
**Ingredients:**
- Tequila: 2 oz
- Creme de Cacao: 0.75 oz
- Lemon Juice: 0.75 oz
- Absinthe: 1 spritz

**Notes:** Stir 10s, strain into glass

---

## 2. Espresso Martini (Chartreuse)
**Ingredients:**
- Espresso: 1 oz
- Vodka: 2 oz
- Coffee liqueur: 0.5 oz
- Simple syrup: 0.25 oz
- Chartreuse Bitters: 1 dash

**Notes:** Shake, double strain. Put espresso in freezer to chill

---

## 3. Japanese Manhattan
**Ingredients:**
- Miyagikyo Single Malt: 2 oz
- Matsui Umeshu: 1 oz
- Vermouth del Professore: 0.5 oz
- Chocolate bitters: 2 dashes
- Angostura bitters: 1 dash

**Notes:** Stir, strain into chilled glass

---

## 4. Sazerac
**Ingredients:**
- Rye Whiskey: 2.5 oz
- Absinthe: 1 spritz
- Sugar: 1 tsp (or 1 sugar cube)
- Cold water: 0.5 tsp
- Angostura Bitters: 1 dash
- Peychaud's Bitters: 4 dashes

**Notes:** Can alternatively use a single sugar cube instead of 1 tsp sugar

---

## 5. Pegu Club
**Ingredients:**
- Gin: 2 oz
- Orange Curaçao: 0.75 oz
- Lime Juice: 0.5 oz
- Angostura Bitters: 1 dash
- Orange Bitters: 1 dash

**Notes:** Shake, double strain

---

## 6. Irish Old Fashioned
**Ingredients:**
- Irish Whisky: 2 oz
- Benedictine: 0.75 oz
- Orange bitters: 2 dashes
- Angostura bitters: 1 dash

---

## 7. Chaiquiri
**Ingredients:**
- Rum: 2 oz
- Chai syrup: 0.75 oz
- Lime: 1 oz
- Angostura bitters: 2 dashes

**Notes:** Small ice

---

## 8. Odango
**Ingredients:**
- Hakutake Kin Shiro rice shochu: 1.5 oz
- Mr Black: 0.33 oz
- Anko: 0.33 oz
- Heavy cream: 1 oz
- Simple syrup: 0.33 oz
- Angostura bitters: 1 dash

**Garnish:** Ground coffee + sweet potato chips

---

## 9. The Federation
**Ingredients:**
- Añejo Tequila: 2 oz
- Absinthe: 1 spritz
- Creme de Cacao: 0.5 oz
- Angostura bitters: 2 dashes

**Garnish:** Orange twist

---
