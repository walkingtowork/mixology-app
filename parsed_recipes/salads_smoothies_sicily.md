# Parsed Drink Recipes - Salads Smoothies Sicily Menu

## 1. Espresso Martini (Rum)
**Ingredients:**
- Espresso: 1 oz
- Rum: 2 oz
- Homemade coffee liqueur: 0.5 oz
- Simple syrup: 0.25 oz

**Notes:** Small ice. Put espresso in freezer to chill. Optionally express lemon or orange peel.

---

## 2. Limoncello Spritz
**Ingredients:**
- Dry Prosecco: 3 oz
- Limoncello: 2 oz
- Soda water: 1 oz

**Garnish:** Orange slice

**Notes:** Small ice. Ice first, briefly stir, garnish with orange slice. Mint if you have it.

---

## 3. Rings a Bell
**Ingredients:**
- Gin: 0.5 oz
- Verde Amaro: 1.5 oz
- Egg white: 0.5 oz
- Lemon: 0.33 oz
- Honey syrup: 1 tsp

**Garnish:** Grated tonka bean

**Notes:** Small ice. 

---

## 4. Italian Buck
**Ingredients:**
- Cynar: 1.5 oz
- Vecchio del Capo: 1.5 oz
- Lime: 0.75 oz
- Ginger Beer: 3 oz

**Garnish:** Lime wheel

**Notes:** Small ice. Shake before adding Ginger Beer, lime wheel garnish. Optional bitters

---

## 5. Hibiscus Sour
**Ingredients:**
- Hibiscus Cinnamon Gin: 1 oz
- Simple Syrup: 1 oz
- Lime: 1 oz
- Egg white: 0.5 oz

**Garnish:** Grated nutmeg (could try ginger or chai syrup instead of simple?)

**Notes:** Ice optional?

---

## 6. Spicy Pineapple Daiquiri
**Ingredients:**
- Rum: 1 oz
- Chili Amaro: 1 oz
- Lime: 0.5 oz
- Pineapple syrup: 0.5 oz

**Notes:** Small ice. More lime if too sweet?

---

## 7. Rancor's Toothpick
**Ingredients:**
- 100-proof Bourbon: 1.5 oz
- Cynar: 1 oz
- Sweet Vermouth: 1 oz
- Mole Bitters: 2 dashes

**Garnish:** Orange peel

**Notes:** Stir and pour over a large block of ice.

---

## 8. Revolver
**Ingredients:**
- Bourbon: 2 oz
- Coffee Liqueur: 0.5 oz
- Orange bitters: 2 dashes

**Garnish:** Flamed orange peel

**Notes:** Ice optional.

---

## 9. Leftovers
**Ingredients:**
- Hibiscus Cinnamon Gin: 1 oz
- Homemade coffee liqueur: 1 oz
- Pineapple syrup: 1 oz
- Lime: 1 oz
