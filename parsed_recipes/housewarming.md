# Parsed Drink Recipes - Housewarming Menu

## 1. Chaiquiri
**Ingredients:**
- Rum: 2 oz
- Chai syrup: 0.75 oz
- Lime: 1 oz

**Notes:** Small ice

---

## 2. Espresso Martini
**Ingredients:**
- Espresso: 1 oz
- Vodka: 2 oz
- Coffee liqueur: 0.5 oz
- Simple syrup: 0.25 oz

**Notes:** Small ice. Put espresso in freezer to chill

---

## 3. Negroni
**Ingredients:**
- Gin: 1 oz
- Vermouth: 1 oz
- Campari: 1 oz

**Garnish:** Orange slice

**Notes:** Large ice

---

## 4. Irish Coffee
**Ingredients:**
- Irish whisky: 1.5 oz
- Hot Coffee: 4 oz
- Sugar: 2 tbsp

**Garnish:** Heavy cream

---

## 5. Gin Fizz
**Ingredients:**
- Gin: 2 oz
- Lemon: 1 oz
- Egg white: 0.5 oz
- Simple syrup: 0.75 oz
- Soda water: ~1 oz (approximately)

**Notes:** Small ice

---

## 6. Black Russian
**Ingredients:**
- Vodka: 2 oz
- Kahlua: 1 oz
- Heavy cream: 0.5 oz

---

## 7. Rum Old Fashioned
**Ingredients:**
- Rum: 2 oz
- Simple syrup: 1 tsp
- Chai syrup: 1 tsp
- Angostura bitters: 2 dashes
- Orange bitters: 2 dashes

**Garnish:** Orange twist

---

## Notes:
- **Gin Fizz - Egg white**: Using 0.5 oz (standard when not specified)
- **Black Russian - Heavy cream**: Using 0.5 oz for "splash"
- **Rum Old Fashioned**: Includes both simple syrup and chai syrup
